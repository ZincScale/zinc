05 — Lore

Zinc is element 30 (Zn) — bluish-silver, used in galvanisation to protect other metals.
AZ-30 references AZ-5, the Chernobyl SCRAM button — atomic number 30, emergency shutdown.
SCRAM is the nuclear term for emergency reactor shutdown.
PANIC is Go's runtime fatal error — since Zinc transpiles to Go, it's the language's own shutdown word.
The badger hunts gophers. Zinc transpiles to Go (the gopher language).
The hazmat suit ties the mascot to the nuclear/reactor aesthetic of the wordmark.
